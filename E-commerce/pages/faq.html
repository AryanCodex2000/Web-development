Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies.

How do I place an order?

+

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping information and payment details to complete your purchase.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

+

You can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it. After that, please contact our customer service team, and we'll do our best to assist you. If the order has already been processed, modifications may not be possible.

What are your shipping options and delivery times?

+

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free on orders over $50
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $9.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $19.99

Delivery times may vary based on your location and current order volume.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. At checkout, you'll see the available shipping options for your country.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be in new, unused condition with all original tags attached. Some items like swimwear, lingerie, and beauty products are final sale and cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I initiate a return or exchange?

+

To initiate a return or exchange, log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the order you want to return and follow the prompts. You can also contact our customer service team for assistance. Return shipping is free for defective items or shipping errors.

How do I determine my size?

+

We provide detailed size charts for each product. You can find these on the product page under the "Size Guide" tab. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit. For specific sizing questions, feel free to contact our customer service team.

Are your products authentic?

+

Yes, all products sold on StyleHub are 100% authentic. We work directly with brands and authorized distributors to ensure the authenticity of all items. We do not sell counterfeit or replica products.

How do I create an account?

+

You can create an account by clicking on the "Account" icon in the header and selecting "Sign Up". You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and enter some basic information. Having an account allows you to track orders, save payment methods, and access your wishlist.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

+

If you've forgotten your password, click on "Sign In" and then select "Forgot Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link will expire after 24 hours for security reasons.

Need More Help?

If you can't find the answer to your question, our customer service team is here to help.
