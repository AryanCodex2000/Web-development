Returns & Exchanges

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Our Return Policy

At StyleHub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a 30-day return policy on most items.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must:

  • Be returned within 30 days of delivery date
  • Be in new, unused condition with all original tags attached
  • Be in original packaging where applicable
  • Not be damaged or soiled

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Swimwear and intimate apparel (if worn or without original packaging)
  • Beauty and skincare products (if opened or used)
  • Gift cards
  • Final sale items (clearly marked as such)
  • Personalized or custom-made items

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your StyleHub account
  2. Go to "My Orders" and select the order you want to return
  3. Choose the items you wish to return and select "Return Items"
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label
  5. Package the items securely and attach the return label
  6. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item. To initiate an exchange:

  1. Follow the return process above
  2. Select "Exchange" instead of "Return for Refund"
  3. Choose your preferred replacement item
  4. We'll ship the replacement once we receive your return

Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Please note:

  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping is free for defective items or shipping errors
  • For other returns, return shipping costs will be deducted from your refund

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will:

  • Arrange for a free return shipping label
  • Process a full refund or replacement
  • Cover all shipping costs

In-Store Returns

You may also return online purchases at any StyleHub retail location. Please bring:

  • The item(s) in original condition
  • Original packing slip or order confirmation
  • Valid photo ID

International Returns

For international orders, return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error or a defective item.
