Shipping Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Shipping Options

StyleHub offers several shipping options to meet your needs:

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days Free on orders over $50
$5.99 for orders under $50
Express Shipping 2-3 business days $9.99
Overnight Shipping 1 business day $19.99
International Shipping 7-14 business days Calculated at checkout

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Processing time may vary during peak periods or promotional events.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Delivery Restrictions

We currently ship to the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, and select European countries. For international shipments, customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address or refusal, we will contact you to arrange redelivery. Additional shipping charges may apply for redelivery attempts.

Shipping to P.O. Boxes

We can ship to P.O. Box addresses using Standard Shipping only. Express and Overnight shipping options are not available for P.O. Box deliveries.

International Shipping

For international orders:

  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight
  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs processing
  • Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees
  • Some items may be restricted for international shipping

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package appears to be lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will work with the shipping carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this shipping policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.
